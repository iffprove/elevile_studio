The Studio

Objects of Eternal Wonder

From a studio in Perth, Australia, Elevile crafts faithful museum-quality replicas of humanity's most extraordinary ancient objects. Each piece begins with 3D scans of original artifacts, cast and hand-finished with obsessive attention to texture, scale, and surface character — Ice Age Venus figurines, paleolithic pebbles that may be the first faces ever recognised, totems so rare their originals have sold for tens of millions. Now in your hands.

The Craft

Made With
Obsessive Care

  • I

    Historical Research

    Each piece begins with deep archival study — museum records, archaeological reports, and primary sources.

  • II

    Faithful Modelling

    Proportions, surface texture, and iconography are rendered to match original artifacts as closely as possible.

  • III

    Hand Finishing

    Every replica receives individual hand-treatment — patination, painting, and aging to achieve authentic character.

  • IV

    Provenance Notes

    Each piece ships with a card documenting its historical origin, culture, date, and significance.

Our Philosophy
To hold the ancient is to remember we are not the first — nor the last

We are a small team of artisans driven by the belief that history should not be sealed behind museum glass. The Venus of Willendorf, carved 25,000 years ago in the Ice Age. The Makapansgat Pebble, picked up by a hominid ancestor 3 million years ago who saw a face in it. These objects mark the earliest moments of human wonder. Elevile exists to put that wonder directly into your hands — faithful reproductions that carry the weight, texture, and presence of the originals.
