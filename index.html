About Elevile

One Artisan.
A Thousand Centuries
of Inspiration.

Elevile is a small studio based in Perth, Australia, run by a single passionate artisan with a deep love for the art and history of ancient civilizations. Every piece is researched, cast, and hand-finished with museum-grade attention to detail.

The shop features replicas drawn from cultures spanning the globe and millennia — from Paleolithic Venus figurines to Mesoamerican guardians, from European stone idols to Pacific island carvings. Each one is crafted to be as true to the original as possible.

Custom orders are welcome. If there is a piece from history you have always wanted to hold, reach out — it can likely be made.
